Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ATS and why does it matter?

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is software used by 99% of Fortune 500 companies to filter and manage job applications. If your resume isn't optimized for ATS parsing, your skills may never be seen by a recruiter — even if you're perfectly qualified.

Is ResumeXray free to use?

Yes! ATS scanning, AI bullet rewrites, and resume analysis are completely free with unlimited usage. You only pay when you download your optimized resume or cover letter, starting at $0.40 per download.

Which ATS platforms does ResumeXray simulate?

ResumeXray simulates parsing behavior from 6+ major ATS platforms including Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, iCIMS, Taleo, and SmartRecruiters.

How does ResumeXray improve my resume?

ResumeXray analyzes your resume's parse rate, format health, and keyword match against your target job description. Our AI then rewrites weak bullet points using the CAR formula (Challenge → Action → Result) and generates an ATS-optimized version of your resume.

1. What We Collect & Cookie Preferences

When you use ResumeXray, we collect:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, hashed password (for local accounts), or OAuth profile (Google).

  • Job Description Data: Text or URLs you provide for JD matching.
  • Usage Data: IP address (hashed), browser type, scan timestamps, feature usage.
  • Payment Data: Processed by Stripe. We never store credit card numbers.

We use cookies to ensure our platform functions securely. Upon your first visit, you will be presented with a preference center to manage these technologies.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core platform functionality (e.g., secure login, session routing, and Stripe payment processing). These cannot be disabled.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: Deployed only with your explicit consent to help us understand aggregate feature usage.

You may withdraw your consent or update your granular cookie preferences at any time via the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer.

3. AI Processing

Your resume text is processed by third-party AI models (OpenAI / Google Gemini) for semantic analysis, bullet optimization, and cover letter generation. We do not train AI models on your personal data.

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

Account data is retained until account deletion. Scan data: 12 months. Guest scan data (IP-based): purged daily.
